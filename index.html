About

Shaitaan" is a gripping Indian thriller directed by Bejoy Nambiar, known for its intense storyline and dark themes. Set in Mumbai, the film follows a group of reckless youngsters who find themselves entangled in a web of crime and deceit after a night of partying goes horribly wrong. As their lives spiral out of control, they must confront their inner demons and face the consequences of their actions.

With its gritty portrayal of urban life and morally ambiguous characters, "Shaitaan" keeps viewers on the edge of their seats from start to finish. The film's atmospheric cinematography and haunting soundtrack add to its suspenseful atmosphere, making it a must-watch for fans of psychological thrillers.
